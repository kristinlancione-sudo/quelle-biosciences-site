About Quelle BioSciences Inc.

For decades, the luxury skincare industry faced an intractable scientific barrier: methylene blue, celebrated for its powerful cosmetic potential, was irrevocably excluded from elegant formulations due to its intense chromophore—a molecule so brilliantly blue it irreparably stained skin, textiles, and surfaces. Despite its transformative potential, the molecule’s fundamental instability and unavoidable transfer rendered it a technological dead end, a fact universally accepted by chemists and formulators worldwide. This challenge was an accepted reality until Quelle BioSciences Inc. developed a patent pending solution that finally enabled its use.

Under the singular vision and scientific expertise of our founder and CEO, Kristin Lancione Miller, Quelle BioSciences has not only solved this problem but has set a new standard for cosmetic science. Our provisional patent outlines a synergistic composition that achieves an 85% reduction in fabric transfer and a 3.2x greater retention of the active ingredient on the skin. This breakthrough delivers the full potency of a powerful molecule in a formula that is truly colorless and elegant. We have effectively redefined what is possible in the world of high-performance skincare.

Quelle BioSciences Inc., led by founder and CEO Kristin Lancione Miller, redefines cosmetic science through pioneering synergistic polymer-dye interactions and advanced light-management systems. Protected by patent pending technology, our innovative platform delivers unmatched stability, elegance, and efficacy, seamlessly integrating potent actives with non-staining, high-performance aesthetics. Backed by rigorous scientific validation, Quelle sets the global benchmark for premium skincare and is poised to lead the industry in next-generation cosmetic innovation.

Our Patent Pending Synergistic Technology

Our patent pending technology combines three key pillars to deliver unprecedented results.

The MB:Polymer Complex

We have discovered a critical synergistic interaction between methylene blue and specific cationic polymers. This complexation reduces free dye migration by 85%, eliminating staining and ensuring the active stays where it's needed.

Advanced Light-Management

Our proprietary system uses micronized titanium dioxide and iron oxides to achieve a clear, non-tinted look on skin. This technology ensures the product is aesthetically neutral while maintaining full efficacy.

Optimized Bioavailability

By controlling the zeta potential of our formulations, we enhance the skin's substantivity. Our technology achieves 3.2x greater retention of methylene blue, ensuring maximum benefits with minimal application.
